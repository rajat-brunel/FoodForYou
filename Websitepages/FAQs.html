How does Food For You work?

It's really quite straightforward - simply enter your postcode on the homepage, and we'll show you all the great restaurants near you. Choose the restaurant that tickles your fancy, maybe one that you have rated before hand, one that people rate highly or even according to price using the filter options. Click on your favoured restaurant and check out the menu - you can then order from delivery provider and enjoy a perfect meal!
