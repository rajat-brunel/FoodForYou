Main functions within Food For You
Location filter  filter within Food For You can be used by customers to rearrange the food accordingly to the location it is based in. As often customers prefer to find a place to eat accordingly to where it is based.This may well be beneficial for any customer using this application as they will be able to have an insight into the different types of cuisines based around them.

Cost filter within Food For You can be used by customers to rearrange the food accordingly to the price. Since our target audience is most likely to be students, they may prefer to search for food depending on the price range of the dish. This may well be beneficial for any customer using this application as they will be able to have an insight into the price range of any particular dish as per their desire. As per our market research, no other application allows a customer to look at dish prices and filter it accordingly to price range.

One of the features of this app is the Reviews. Many customers look at the food apps and want to enjoy their food more than anything, money is a strong aspect but a lot people are willing to pay more for quality and taste, this is why reviews are important, it allows people to see what others have rated the restaurant as well as any particular comments or opinions they had of the food, this allows you to pick the right and preferable cuisine for yourself.
